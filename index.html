P BOM :

This process describes the procedure for preparing “Preliminary Bill of Material” (also called P-BOM)

This is prepared, during initial design Proposal stage, for purpose, like estimating the approx. cost of the product, to access the work content and time for development of the product etc.

Based on specific request with details from Marketing, in TCP or as discussed and cleared from Sales Project meeting and subsequently regularised in TCP.

Design Review :

This process describes the methods and actions on Design Review, conducted for New projects.

Normally this design review to be conducted at various stages.

i.e., Before start of design, Before prototypes drawing release ,,Before production drawing release.
